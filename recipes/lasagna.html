Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • meat
  • tomatoes
  • cheese
  • pasta

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the meat over medium heat until browned.
  3. Add the tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, until bubbly and golden.
